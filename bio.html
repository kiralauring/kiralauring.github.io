About Me

Hello!
I am a sophomore at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and American Studies with a concentration in Institutions and Power in the U.S. concerning race and disability. I want to use tech to make the world a more accessible and unisolating place!

At Tufts, I am head of design at Currents Magazine, the editor of the Tufts Observer's Voices section, and an intern at the Tufts University Women's Center.

In my free time I love to dance, make books and zines, and cook!
